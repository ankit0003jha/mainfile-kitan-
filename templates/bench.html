TYPES OF BENCH VISE

METAL-WORKING VISE

Metalworking vise or machinists vise is the ultimate choice for engineers’. That’s why it is also known as Engineer’s vise. This one is used to clamp metal in place of wood.

Pipe vises are used for holding a pipe. It is a must for plumbers. Pipe vises are important while cutting or threading a pipe. Pipe vises are also sturdy and useful for both soft and heavy-duty workings.

HAND VISE

Hand vises are most commonly used in various workplaces or in the garage of hobbyists. You can use it easily in your hand or fasten it with the bench vise. It is used to get a grip on softer things.
