TYPES OF SCREW-DRIVERS

SLOTTED SCREW-DRIVER

Slot screw drives have a single horizontal indentation (the slot) in the fastener head and is driven by a "common blade" or flat-bladed screwdriver.

PHILLIPS SCREW-DRIVER

A screwdriver that is designed to be used with a type of screw (called a Phillips-head screw) that has a slot in its top that looks like a cross.

TORX SCREW-DRIVER

Torx is a trademarked type of screw drive characterized by a 6-point star-shaped pattern. A popular generic name for the drive is star, or star bits.

HEX SCREW-DRIVER

Sometimes you can use small types of flathead screwdrivers as a hex wrench by putting the end in the socket so that the two edges of the screwdriver work as leverage in the hole to turn it.
